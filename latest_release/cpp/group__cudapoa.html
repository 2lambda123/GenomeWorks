◆ BandMode

enum claraparabricks::genomeworks::cudapoa::BandMode

Banding mode used in Needleman-Wunch algorithm full_band performs computations on full scores matrix, highest accuracy static_band performs computations on a fixed band along scores matrix diagonal, fastest implementation adaptive_band, similar to static_band performs computations on a band along diagonal, but the band-width can vary per alignment's score matrix, faster than full_band and more accurate than static_band
